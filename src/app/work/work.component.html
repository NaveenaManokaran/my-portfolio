Operational Dashboard

Operational Dashboard is one among the types of dashboard used by management to monitor and manage the operations that have a short period to maintain accurate and fast data between the department and operational team. It is a reporting tool for monitoring business requirements that most often change and to keep track. The primary goal is to provide real-time information and the management need not wait for the analysis report in a spreadsheet. (read more..)

Agile Resource Tool

Agile Resource Tool involves splitting projects into incremental phases so that project teams can deliver completed pieces of a project on a routine. To be more specific, It is a tool to gather requirements, plan, design, develop, release, and track & monitor the project. The goal of the agile tool is to decrease waste and provide more opportunities for client feedback and team improvement. (read more..)

New year relevance tool

The new year relevance tool is a tool for managing the application throughout the fiscal year along with their execution order of parent task and their dependent child task based on the Priority (schedule). i.e., Maintaining and executing all the applications and its relevant tasks categorized under each fiscal Year. The main goal of this tool is to avoid deadlock situations and inaccuracy of the data. (read more..)
